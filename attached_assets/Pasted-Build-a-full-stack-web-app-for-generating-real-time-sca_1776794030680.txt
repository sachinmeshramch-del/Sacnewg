🧠 MARKET MODE DETECTION

TRENDING:

- EMA20 above EMA50 → bullish trend
- EMA20 below EMA50 → bearish trend

SIDEWAYS:

- EMA20 and EMA50 are flat and close together
- Price moving in small range

---

⚙️ SIGNAL ENGINE (SMART SCORING)

🟢 BUY SCORE

- EMA20 > EMA50 → +30
- Price above EMA20 → +20
- MACD bullish crossover → +20
- RSI between 50–65 → +15 (avoid overbought)
- ATR rising → +15

🔴 SELL SCORE

- EMA20 < EMA50 → +30
- Price below EMA20 → +20
- MACD bearish crossover → +20
- RSI between 35–50 → +15
- ATR rising → +15

---

🎯 SIGNAL DECISION

- BUY if score ≥ 65
- SELL if score ≥ 65
- Else HOLD

---

🔁 SIDEWAYS MARKET LOGIC (VERY IMPORTANT FOR GOLD)

- RSI < 30 → BUY (bounce)
- RSI > 70 → SELL (reversal)
- TP small (quick scalp)

---

⚡ ENTRY CONFIRMATION (CRITICAL FOR GOLD)

  - Bullish candle close
  - Break of previous high
- SELL:
  - Bearish candle close

🛑 RISK MANAGEMENT (GOLD SPECIFIC)
